Honoo no Haramase Doukyuusei is a follow up to Honoo no Haramase Tenkousei. In this instance we meet Kazuya Kagami, the cousin of Tatsuya Tagami who was the male lead in the previous title. Inspired by his cousin's success at impregnating all 21 of his classmates, Kazuya decides he wants to do it as well. Like his cousin, Kazuya is the only male student in his class and lives as the only male at his school's dormitory. He starts out his plan of impregnating all 20 of his female classmates by seducing his childhood friend Asuka. From there he moves on to each of the other girls, taking their virginities with raw sex and doing his best to sow his seed as he goes.

There are two basic facts that make this second outing inferior to the first one. First off, it is only has two episodes instead of three. Second it the amount of time wasted introducing each of the girls to give a small bio of each one. It felt like half the show was just introductions. Also, with 20 girls to go through in just two episodes, and the fact that he mostly focused on each girl independently instead of in groups like the first series, the hentai content lacked any reasonable scene length and it seemed that most of the time was spent in transition rather than action.

The artwork is very nice and the female characters are all hot and well designed. The animation is smooth, crisp and well done. As with the previous title, my only complaint is the level of censorship; it is just poorly done and dominates the scenes. They might have overdone the sound effects a bit, but would not say its bad. The redeeming factors for this title is the fact that there are a lot of very nice ecchi chicks and just enough humor to make it interesting.

Conclusion: this is not a bad hentai at all, in fact it is very enjoyable. I just wish they had taken a bit more time with the content and paid more attention to each of the girls. This is worth the watch, but not as good as its predecessor.

Kagami Kazuya is a high school boy who dreams of world domination. The only thing he can do to attempt such a feat is to have sex with every girl and add them to his harem. Since the school he attends only recently became co-ed and Kazuya is surrounded by 20 beautiful girls in his class, he decides to start by conquering all of them and then move on from there with all of them helping him.

And then he does just that. This two episode hentai consists of around five minutes at the beginning of each episode with just a little nudity and sex, but most of this part is used to introduce the girls who Kazuya will be conquering in that episode. Then the rest of the time is spent showing him conquering girls easily at rapid fire speed with only a couple difficulties along the way. Nine girls in the first episode and eleven girls in the second basically guarantee that there will be at least one girl who interests you. However, with the large cast, each of the sex scenes are fairly short and many contain two or three girls being conquered together. Still, having them back to back with almost no break in between keeps it from losing its effect. The only real problem with the quick scenes comes if you really don't like one of them (which will probably happen at some point due to the wide variety of female archetypes, positions, and ideas for how to make each scene a little different).

The girls all fall into a specific archetype and none of them were really meant to be anything more than that. Everything from a rich blonde girl (with that annoying pompous laugh) to an android to a strict tsundere. The girls all sound great though it's not much of a surprise that the voice actresses tend to play multiple roles (though I didn't notice it while watching since I didn't see the episodes at the same time). The background music is good and mainly consists of fast paced guitar riffs.

Honoo no Haramase: Doukyuusei is a good harem hentai as long as you don't mind short, back to back sex scenes each with their own different archetypal girls and different positions. Both of the episodes are equally good and they both had a few scenes that stood out to me while there was maybe one in each that I didn't care much for. Watch it and see for yourself which scenes you like the best.
