Synopsis

Kazuhiro Sayama finally gathers the courage to confess to his crush, Erika Sakurai. She responds by immediately having sex with their gym teacher in front of him. It turns out that the girl of his dreams is a voracious sex addict and she's especially into exhibitionism.

The two strike a deal: Kazuhiro will become Erika's personal cameraman, filming every one of her sexual encounters. He gets to watch the girl he likes in every lewd situation imaginable, with only one catch—he himself may not have sex with her. Will Kazuhiro be content with their arrangement, or will he shoot for something more?

Kanojo wa Dare to demo Sex Suru is a pretty basic anime.

Story:
One of the most basic stories I have ever watched. The ending was quite predictable since there was so much build up to it. There was no filler so I was not able to 'get to know' the characters enough for me to call this a 'relatable' show. Overall a 3/10

Sound:
Aside from the sloppy sound of you know what going into you know what, the sound was quite appalling. However, I dont think the anime had that many opportunities when it comes to sound. I would have prefered maybe: Action scenes (swords clashing), maybe car racing? Im not sure. This anime lacked in sound overall and made the anime more unenjoyable.

Characters:
The main focus of the show, Erika, is quite a weird human being. She enjoys being filmed on camera having sex, which in my opinion: is quite a normal kink. She lacks any emotions aside from horniness and as the show carries on, this is further supported. Kazuhiro Sayama, is a laughable character. He's in love with a crazed girl who's only focus is having sex with complete strangers. Clearly, he should be looking for someone else to focus on.

Art:
I dont need to say anything for this. This aspect of the show was a masterpiece. 10/10

Overall, I must say that the anime was quite decent due to the miraculous art style. I did not enjoy myself too much, though. The lack of depth to the characters really brought the show down for me and did not allow me to enjoy the show for what it truly was.

However, if you're looking for a shallow anime which is all about lust for sex and jealousy, this anime is for you!
