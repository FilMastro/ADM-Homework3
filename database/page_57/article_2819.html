My re-watch of the Sekirei saga continues with this episode. So, was it any good? Well, let's hop on and find out, shall we?

Story
The show takes a short break from the whole Sekirei battle royale and places all our favorite characters in so many ecchi scenarios that would make the characters from Highschool DxD blush in embarrasment. Ecchi is the name of this episode's game. There isn't much content that advances the story of Sekirei and this episode exists simply to provide as many ecchi scenes as possible. (6/10)

Characters
Aside from the characters reacting to all the ecchi stuff happening around them, there isn't much in the way of good character writing here. I mean, the characters have always been concerned with beauty and physical attractiveness, so, it would make sense that the characters would compare their breasts to each other. (5/10)

Art
The fanservice was this episode's meat and potatoes, so it was imperative for this episode to be as visually stunning as possible. (7/10)

Sound
Enough praise cannot go to the voice actors in this series, both in its Japanese and English versions. The opening and ending songs weren't bad, either. (7/10)

Overall
Although it was titled as the episode 0 for the second season, this episode doesn't contain anything relevant for said season, so you don't exactly need to watch it. That said, it's still fun and hilarious and I definitely recommend it. (6/10)
