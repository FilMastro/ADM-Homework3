What can i say, this is your typical ecchi OVA filled with fanservice. When i watched the OVA it was listed as episode 13 but i couldn't really fit the episode in the after story so i think this is just a side story, there's no plot development there's no new story, this is purely fanservice.

I'll assume you've watched the series before reading this review. Since the synopsis doesn't have any information about the story i'll just say it's your typical highschool swimsuit contest situation between the 4 main female characters. You can imagine what comes next since all of them are trying to win over Ryosuke and he's trying to help Lisara and the one who organized it all was of course Iria.

Overall this is a nice addition to the series if you're into the whole fanservice thing, the structure is the same as all the episodes so there's nothing new you'll see here, just enjoy all the bouncing breasts and panty shots you probably loved from the series.

As my grandpa always says: "An ecchi isn't quite complete until there's a swimsuit episode."

Story: 2/10
This is your typical run-of-the-mill swimsuit episode! The 4 main females in a swimsuit contest. So the story this contributes to the original series= none! Personally I loved the series and I actually thought the original series had good story despite the obvious fan-service that was going on. So if you want that swimsuit episode as bad as my grandpa, this OVA is for you.

Art: 10/10
Art is great... if you know what I mean.

Sound: 10/10
A perfect 10 here. The corresponding musics are good. The intro and outro music are the same as the original, and I personally have them on my playlist. Its all the same and is good.

Character: 10/10
Lisara and Ryousuke and all the other character's hold up and you don't get a ruined vision of them by watching this OVA.

Enjoyment: 8/10
If you come into this OVA expecting a fan service only episode, you are getting everything you should be expecting. And your enjoyment will vary with how much you like fan service. I liked it, and I'm happy I experienced this episode, mainly because of my grandpa's wise words.
