"Aneki, My Sweet Elder Sister", despite the name, is one of the most vanilla hentai titles around. The four episode OVA adaptation features short stories of straight young couples either having impromptu sexual encounters, or falling in love and then sexing it up. Excluding the first couple, there's no follow-up on the stories, and the subsequent encounters are more straight-forward. A couple stories focus on incest and more fetish stuff, but the rest of them can be characterized as vanilla. For hentai standards, the sex is quite consensual, but some coercion comes into play here and there.

Animation here is the best you'll find among hentai. Aneki boasts the most jaw-dropping art after Kangoku Senkan, in my opinion. The art style is distinctly recognizable: The girls are fleshy, their breasts and asses detailed and the perpetually erect nipples look juicy. A lot of camera shots are spent just showing the girls' moist puffy lips, pushing you to associate their mouths with their wet pussies.

You can seldom trust the cover art of any erotic content, but in the case of Aneki, the DVD cover is actually representative of the material. I seriously applaud the producers T-Rex and Pink Pineapple for their honesty.

Watching this title you feel warm inside, because every moment of it just bursts with real, carnal passion. I'm surprised the people drawing this were able to finish it, seeing how much they obviously enjoyed it.
