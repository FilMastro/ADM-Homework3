Synopsis

Takashi has a loving girlfriend, but when it comes time to do the dirty deed, he finds himself unable to perform. On a train ride following this incident, Takashi discovers his teacher, Misaki, getting touched by a subway groper. Strangely though, instead of getting upset, Misaki seems to be enjoying it. And much to his own surprise, Takashi does too.

Thus begins Takashi’s journey as a pupil to his teacher in a most carnal sense when they meet on the train every morning. As he explores his desires with his teacher, Takashi becomes a more confident lover. But will his new knowledge help Takashi become closer to his beloved girlfriend? Or scare her away?

This just... wow. I was blown away by this one. Hi.me.go.to is undoubtedly one of the most refined hentai OVAs I've ever watched (and yes, I just used "hentai" and "refined" in the same sentence). I cannot even put into words how much I enjoyed this one.

Plot (9/10): The premise of the story is actually rather appealing. Boy wants to give it to the girl, but can't get his member hard, so he goes to an expert (i.e. the teacher) for lessons. This story did a rather good job of maintaining a teacher-student relationship between Takashi and Ms. Misaki, where she mentors and coaches him through the subtle art of pleasing a woman. Once she gets her car fixed and stops riding the train, Takashi finds his girlfriend Tomomi again, and demonstrates everything Ms. Misaki taught him. From the moment they are close together on the train to the end of their sex in the restroom, he touches her in all the right places. :)

Art (10/10): The art in this hentai was... my god... the art in this hentai was amazing, in a word. All of the characters were drawn in an appealing style, and the animation was incredibly fluid and believable.

Sound (10/10): This hentai made alternate use of soft, light, romantic music and quiet, mischievous, playful music, both done largely on piano. This combination set the perfect atmosphere in each scene. A bit of a foreword before I go any further: For me, the intercourse noises in a hentai play a MAJOR role in how much it arouses me. That being said, this hentai deployed the perfect intercourse sounds-- they weren't too loud, nor too soft, they had just the right amount of wetness, and they still came off as convincing.

Character (10/10): The characters in this hentai were perfectly crafted. Takashi, the quiet, reserved, yet realistically red-blooded male protagonist was perfectly complemented by the demure yet secretly naughty Ms. Misaki and the sweet, meek, adorable Tomomi.

Enjoyment (9/10): The only reason I don't give this a full 10/10 is because of one minor flaw the hentai snagged on-- the animators chose a rather large, imposing, uniform circular pixel/blur censor to cover up the genitalia. However, it also covered up much of the surrounding area every time it appeared (which also would happen rather abruptly when genitalia were exposed). It wasn't exactly the best choice for censoring, but it wasn't so bad that it detracted in any significant way from the experience this hentai gave me.

Overall (10/10): Hi.me.go.to is, without a doubt, a masterpiece in hentai. I'm definitely keeping this one in my favorites collection.

Tenouttaten. Would fap again.

This Hentai is odd in that the sex scenes provided character growth for the male protagonist. The female protagonist is also not the initial love interest. We see these two protagonists offering each offering something during their time together. Although the female protagonist does enjoy sex which is provided by the male protagonist, there is also the notion that she is actually a kind lover and teacher beyond her role as part of the school faculty. She coaches him in sex and he gains confidence through their numerous liaisons. One might also argue that they could also potentially become a couple, but the end does suggest that he might have made the decision to be with his love interest. Is the female protagonist likely to rely on their interactions to be happy? No, but there did appear to be chemistry there in spite of the age difference and power dynamic.
To the question of whether or not the male protagonist is cheating... I would argue that this is up in the air given that his relationship was effectively put on hold during his time with the teacher. We basically do not see the pair interact at all during this time, even though his love interest is pining and worrying about him. That suggests that he has taken time off to work on himself.
All in all, this is one of the most tastefully done Hentai that I have ever seen. Given the focus, it seemed more of an explicit coming of age story about a young man's sexuality. This made it more enjoyable for me personally, and something that seems to have annoyed a few viewers that have posted feedback in the forums and on some other websites.
