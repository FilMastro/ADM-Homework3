Tags: virgin, loli, school, rape, mind break, mind control, creampie, anal, paizuri, hallucinations

Story:

As this is a 4 episode story i'll be breaking it down a bit for each arc (this will contain spoilers, but it's hentai we don't exactly watch it for its story)

Episode 1: starts of with sumire-chan A stoic honor student who is borderline emotionless, until one day she is molested on a train and "saved" by her sensei (her class mates usually brag about how much they love being molested on trains, weirdest hentai logic I've ever seen) under advisement of her sensei she gets counseling from the school, however the counselor has a different kind of counseling in mind, queue the mind control based rape of a loli...

Episode 2: Continues on from where episode 1 left off with sumire-chan wondering why she's having such weird and vivid "dreams" and the counselor continuing to rape her, mid way through however her sensei walks in on the counselor having sex with sumire-chan and she passes out when she comes to she is being raped by her sensei, as it turns out she was being recorded every time she had sex, she wasn't dreaming and the entire thing was an elaborate setup by the sensei to turn her into his sexslave. After a few scenes of him raping her against her will she mind breaks and falls into depravity, after this happens the counselor suggests something that could bring them even more pleasure, wiping her memories so that they could start over as a couple.

Episode 3: Starting off with sensei and sumire-chan in a innocent relationship (honestly thought this was going to turn into some slightly depraved vanilla hentai at this point, and honestly i think it would've been better if it had) for some unknown reason sensei is transferring and won't be able to see her for 2-3 years (why?!?!) after he leaves the counselor triggers a hypnosis and then begins to rape her all over again (the hallucinations of this are she gets caught naked on a train by a class mate and gets exploited into being gangraped over and over again by boys in her class while the girls watch)

Episode 4: the gangrape hallucinations continue and then sumire-chan wants to get revenge, so the counselor "sets it up" so that she can watch them get raped on the train like she had been (all a hallucination) then she blacks out, waking up with sensei groping her, it appears shes blacked out to graduation, she embraces sensei and they have passionate sex (in actuality it's another hallucination) the story ends with the counselor stating that he better take care of sumire-chan till sensei returns.

Art:

Pink pineapples standard for 2009 not anything special, but better than most

Sound:

OST is nice and forgettable which is what you want in a hentai, the VA's all do a very good job of making the characters believable and Sakurai Harumi does a fantastic job as sumire-chan showing very good depth switching between emotionless, to scared, to innocently loving it and finally embracing and becoming a nympho

Character:

The story has 3 MC and some backing characters
Sumire-chan: the female MC initially an emotionless girl whom after repeatedly being raped becomes a nympho, she's easily the most interesting character with the number of changes she has throughout the series
The Counselor: A mysterious man with the medical know-how to make sumire hallucinate, submit to sex and erase her memories.. he is the main rapist in the story
Sensei: A man sumire-chan has a crush on, he is initially painted as her hero, however it is later revealed that he is partnered with the counselor in the plot to mind break sumire
Her classmates: Although they appear through hallucination mainly, they are all perverts bent on making the honor student sumire-chan become a cum dumpster

Enjoyment:

I watched Natsumushi the alternate setting to this hentai that i greattly enjoyed (even though i usually dislike this genre), so my expectations where high, however after watching this i can't help but feel disappointed at this train-wreck of a story with some what average scenes, if it had gone the hardcore vanilla rout from ep 3 on-wards i'd probably be giving the enjoyment an 8 or 9 sadly that's not the case

Overall:

overall hitoriga is A prime example of hentai producer to afraid to change genres to better there production value, however if you are into the rape and mind break genre with 0 care on story than this might be for you... as I primarily prefer vanilla hentai

TL;DR emotionless loli girl gets raped repeatedly by her sensei and hallucinates being gangbanged by her classmates over and over again.
