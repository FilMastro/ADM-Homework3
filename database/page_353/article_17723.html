Synopsis

HHH Triple Ecchi is a collection of three short stories, depicting the unexpected lavish sexual lives of mundane individuals. Each revels in the truly mesmerizing physique of young women and their wild erotic adventures.

Shigure Hen and Shigure Hen Okawari

Shigure Takashima is the role model of a perfect high schooler. A charming student council president, she excels in sports and prides herself on countless academic achievements. Being born into a wealthy family, what else could she wish for?

When Komatsu Kita catches her ferociously masturbating in an empty classroom after school hours, she instantly turns to him for sexual gratification. A relationship soon blooms between the two—one devoted purely to the seemingly insatiable desires of young adults.

Konami Hen

When Konami invites her neighbor over for dinner, little does she realize that the person she views as an older brother is actually haunted by sexual fantasies about her. He simply can not get his eyes off her well-endowed chest, and when Konami falls asleep, he decides to take advantage of her.

Miyuki Hen

Despite being an adult, Miyuki continues to bathe with her step-father. Oblivious as she may be, her parent's true intentions are crystal clear. After all, how can anyone resist such a scrumptious young body, displayed in its glorious entirety? The bath soon turns into something few families use it for.

"Masterpiece" is a word I rarely give out or use for hentai's. The simple fact is I feel hentai is medium more than any other that is constantly been making the same mistakes for decades. At it's conception, hentai's for the most part knew what made a compelling story/scenario to invest an audience fully, but lacked the technological fortitude to bring these idea's to life. Now however, it seems to be quite the opposite. Hentai as a medium is very capable of amazing art and animation but a lot of hentai seem to just half-ass their story and characters for more shmex scenes and think it will result in a better product, which let me say it does not. This is why I am weary when using the word "Masterpiece", and that is why only 4 hentai's on my entire MAL have that golden, reserved rating. One of those hentai's is "HHH Triple Ecchi".

Story: 9/10.
HHH Triple Ecchi is a self-contained episode by episode type of hentai. Meaning, that one episode will have a completely different setting and characters compared to another episode, and this works out excellently for the series. Each episode has good premises for each character that makes no two episode feel the same and each has good development of every character involved.The only reason as to why the story is not a 10/10 for me is due to how it's structure in nature is a bit weak compared to another hentai of similar structure: "Joshikousei no Koshitsuki". This is not however undermining the beauty of it's structure, but having something (such as a common setting) to tie the story together would have been nice.

Art and Animation: 10/10.
Mary Jane has animated this work and at this point they clearly know what they are doing. The art is dare I say perfect. It has very good proportions of characters without it being too jarring for the audience and the color palette is amazing. It stands out enough to the point where the praise is justified but not overall saturated to where it is borderline eye rape. The animation is also very good. It is very smooth with it being very apparent of the time that went in, and the animation clearly utilizes the shorter episode time to its best. Finally the choreography. Just like everything else, there is nothing to worry about! The choreography is really good! It ustiizes a good variety of shot compositions at the right time (such as full body perseptives, organ zooms, boob close-ups... all the good stuff ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°))

Sound: 10/10.
Most hentai's have no problem with their sound direction and HHH Triple Ecchi is no different. The VAs are amazing, the background music is wisely chosen and not too loud that is is distracting and overall it is just great!

Character: 10/10.
All (three) of the female characters in HHH Triple Ecchi are amazing. By far the most popular and easily the most developed character (with a last name) is Takashima, Shigure, who appears in episode 1 and 4. Great touch of ending the series with the character that started it. She is the common stereotypical student council representative who has a horny side that gets exposed to a boy. What really makes her special and one of the best characters in all of hentai is her character shift. She goes from being the dominating shmex partner to the complete opposite, and trust me when I say... it makes sense. Her character arc and development are easily one of the best in all of hentai and her contribution alone deserves praise! Plus her design is fucking amazing! Big tiddes for the win! Konomi is the girl that appears in the second episode and as much as I love her she is easily the weakest out of the three. Konomi is a great character in terms of design and personality, but speaking realistically she gets next to no development by the end of her episode is easily the most forgettable in terms overall appearance. Now on the final and one of my guilty favorites... Miyuki. Why is she a guilty favorite you ask? I'll explain don't worry. It is common knowledge that Takashima, Shigure is the best character in HHH Triple Ecchi, however I still can't help but put Miyuki at the top of the pedestal for the best. Miyuki has the most memorable and realistic portal of an archetype in hentai. If you could not tell her basic personality can be described as a Tsundere, but she is so much more than that as she destroys this common archetype and reforms it into her own. It's something I would rather you experience then tell. But her arc of finally accepting who she likes and moving from her mother's death is a great and captivating thing to witness, which is why I hold her with such high regards.

Enjoyment: 10/10.
HHH Triple Ecchi is one of my favorite hentai's of all time and easily has one of my favorite casts of all time (including Konomi). The story for each episode is great, the art direction is fricking amazing and contains what I believe two of the best shmex scenes in hentai (Miyuki's shower shmex and Takashima's last shmex scene).

Overall: 10/10.
I don't think I need to say anything else. Just go watch this god-damn hentai will you! That's all I can say and I think the fact that this is one of my longest hentai review yet says how much I love this series!

HHH Triple Ecchi is like a satisfying snack, it doesn't demand anything from the viewer but gives back exactly what you want in a small form package. Packing in 3 different plot lines over 4 episodes the show gives us 3/4 good episodes which is definitely a pass in my books with the two episode plot being the best of the three. Watching this series does not require a big time commitment and isn't as emotionally draining as some *cough* Euphoria *cough* providing us with what it says upfront without leaving much of a bad aftertaste in the viewer's morality.


Note : From my source, episode 1 and 4 had the connected plot while episode 2 and 3 had standalone plots

Overall Story Score : 6

Story 1 : 7

For a two episode stint, the frontpage plot for this series was absolutely delightful focusing on our two main characters putting them into situations that contrasted on our female lead's public appearance adding more motivation for her to break free from the constraints and expectations society placed on her. The dynamic between the two leads, especially the sexual power struggle that leaned both ways throughout the story was extremely compelling from an entertainment standpoint and reflected the direction the plot was going in which helped keep the narrative feeling cohesive and clear to the viewer.

Story 2 : 6

Basically your usual childhood friend next door plot where our MC suddenly realizes "how big she's grown. Wow I never noticed she's become a woman I've always seen her as a little girl" and it goes on from there. Nothing special to say however the comedic direction present in this entire series was very much present here elevating an otherwise mundane plot with a couple chuckles. Definitely nothing to write home about however it kept things fresh enough to not be boring.

Story 3 : 5

Of the three plots, this one had the creepiest vibes which weirded me out the most. Overall I've seen my fair share of creepy hentai from the horrifying vore and rape to the humiliation and mind break, while this was nowhere near that level it was the most boring of the three which is far more offensive in a medium built on fapping but also entertainment. The fact that it was dead center in the region of creepy hentai meant it was nothing outrageous but was just subtle enough to bother me mentally. Furthermore it was a cut and dry as you can get with it's taboo play and there was no chemistry between the father and daughter making the dialogue feel completely disconnected. The slight offset from normalcy made the watch experience just uncomfortable enough that it failed to satisfy the bare minimum of what hentai is supposed to do


Art : 7

The art and animation of this show was probably in the upper echelons of production value for the time it was released in. Characters did not any outrageously bad designs and some artistic choices probably from the original source drove the comedy which was the brightest aspect of the show. The characters also had different characteristics and overall designs varied so it didn't feel lazy and effort was put into building a populated world not solely centered on our leads. Especially in b-shots and backgrounds, the art never fell way beyond the expected level of quality from this kind of project and never took the viewer out of the experience. The animation was top notch in facilitating the comedy that was situational based and relied on the visuals to make the viewer laugh, the animation did not let me down in this aspect and the visual comedy was definitely there.

Character : 8

For the two stories I enjoyed, the characters were really what kept me watching. Especially for our first story Shigure and Komatsu had great chemistry in their dialogue and you could feel that a budding relationship was developing organically between them. The back and forth dialogue between them offered comedic moments in the show and as mentioned before helped the viewer follow who held the power. For the second story, it was mainly a one man show of the Onii-chan while it felt as if Konami was braindead during most of the scene till she realized she actually had a voice. MC had a couple visual gags that played well with the situation he found himself in which kept me entertained. For our third story, personally the father figure rubbed me the wrong way and the daughter's role was too passive much like Konami barely contributing to the situation she was involved in.

Like I said at the start, HHH Triple Ecchi was made to appeal to the mass audience. It's a simple snack you can pick up whenever and probably won't think about much afterwards, well except for the CG Komatsu sperm which will haunt my dream for the foreseeable future. It's another safe bet that won't offend you too much and even if it does it won't hammer at your morality or humanity in anyway that any other average hentai would. This vanilla-esque approach is probably why it is a decently popular hentai and can be picked up by your average weeblet.
