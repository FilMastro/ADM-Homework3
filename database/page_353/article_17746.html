Synopsis

At long last, Kayoko Akizuki, the mother of Airi and Marina, has returned home to her husband, Kouzou. Having realized her daughters have spent too long with only one parent, she is determined to mend the family relations. However, little does she know that Kouzou has had a sexual affair with his two stepdaughters for quite some time now.

As time goes by, Kayoko grows increasingly more inquisitive and seems to be finally becoming aware of the situation. Kouzou becomes desperate and aggressive in his courting, taking his stepdaughters in their sleep or in public spaces. Moreover, a new challenger for his attention arrives—further jeopardizing Airi's relationship with her stepfather.

If you couldn't tell there was a big hiatus for my reviews after I watched 'Oni Chichi Re-born'. I did not like it as much as the fans and for some reason it sent me into a dark spiraling hole of wanting to be done with the show so I wouldn't need to watch it but also made me not want to watch it because I DON'T WANT TOO. So my predicament finally met it's end when I decided to 'attempt' through marathon all of Rebuild for this review. Now these reviews do not get me much attention. Most people have already seen the Oni Chichi series and don't bother to go out of their way to read a review criticizing said show. But for the people who do read all my reviews... I guarantee I have not lost my marbles and that I am just about ready as I will ever be to review the fourth season of this series known as: 'Oni Chichi Rebuild".

Story: 4/10.
The story is the most awkward part about this season. I feel the constant flaw I had with each episode was that 'the whole drug situation' never seemed like anything more than a pointless 'gimmick' that didn't need to exist. Well apparently PoRo knew I would complain about this 7 something years later and tired to fix this issue by tieing it in with this season's plot... Did it work? ummmmm not really. Look I am happy that they tried to make it have more. But like the presentation of 'the drugs' in this season really just makes it seem like a seven year old acting like he is smart ass even tho he is not... I like it when hentai tries to make the story interesting enough for me to stop my fap, but like this made me stop for a completely different reason... because of laughter... like the plot in this gets 1 irony point for being so fucking stupid. But what this show still does IS IT FUCKING COCK TEASES YOU. There are so many time in this season when something interesting that never happened in a hentai could occur... but then the creators are like "fuck it... it'll be too unrealistic"... EVEN THOUGH EVERYTHING HAPPENING IN THIS HENTAI IS FUCKING UNREALISTIC!

Art and Animation: 8/10.
Got nothing to say... so far PoRo has been very consistent with these. Something I did notice that I didn't really notice before is how 'slow' the animation feels. And I don't mean that in a: 'They need to put more frames'... I mean it in a: "The animation is fucking dope... but why is the movement so slowwwww....

Sound: 9/10.
YEAH... I GOT NOTHING... IT'S STILL GOOD I GUESS??

Character: 6/10.
The characters are defiantly the most likable they have been since S1. I would give this a 7 but the problem is this character change happens in the last episode... mean while in the other two episodes it just stays the way I dislike it... Airi was the character I had the biggest problems with since her character was radically changing personalities to the point where I question if she has a mental disease... But by the end I can safely say I started liking her character again. And beleieve it or not... this season finally made me not hate Sana! Oh and Yuka is ok I guess. This is the highest enjoyment rating I have given for this show... and frankly it is because of the last episode. Look... I don't think I'll ever be an Oni Chichi fan... But I can safely say this season has made me look forward for Oni Chichi: Refresh♥. Also I'm just happy that I don't have to sit thorough 30 fucking minute episodes for the new season... so yeah. And I do have a confession to make... my scores for the past Oni ChiChi seasons were inflated as fuck. So here is an updated scoring... P.S this season is the best for me so far: Oni Chichi: 6/10. Re-birth: 3/10. Re-born: 5/10.

Overall: 6/10.
Overall it was the most enjoyable ride I had so far from the series... let's just hope this continues!

Oni Chichi Rebuild is the 4th Oni Chichi season and I'll be doing a quick review. It is just as great as its prequel Reborn, if not even better. :D

Most of you are probably familiar with the story behind Oni Chichi. In addition to Airi, Marina, and their father Takami, the plot has expanded to include Airi and Marina's mom Kayako and a new girl called Yuka. Sweeeeet :P

What I LOVE about Rebuild is it combines both elements of the Original and Reborn. It has the same CUTE humor from Reborn while also the RAPE feeling from the Original.

Rebuild started off like Reborn. The girls accepts Papa for what he is, a horny pervert. Papa was fairly easygoing as he cuddles up to have sex with Airi who light-heartedly defies him, but accepts cause she knows he can't hold back his pervertedness. There are several light-hearted sex scenes with Airi and Papa which includes swimsuit scenes and ALL are very well done.

But then Papa goes on a rampage after Airi accuses him of cheating on a new girl. He forces himself on Airi, being very rough, and it returned to the dark side of Oni Chichi. This super hooked me in cause he's gonna punish the disobedient Airi. The contrast of Papa's attitude made Papa's dark side that much more pulsing to watch.

Episode 1 ended in a great cliffhanger. Rebuild definitely has the potential to be even better than Reborn. I absolutely cannot wait for episode 2 :DDD
