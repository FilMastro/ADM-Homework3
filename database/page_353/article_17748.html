Synopsis

Masato Majime has never been the popular type. Addicted to pornography, he has always been perceived as a weirdo and avoided by most people. Everything changes, however, when he saves a mysterious stray cat. It turns out that the cat is able to speak, and as a token of gratitude, she gives Majime the "Smartphone of Love." Equipped with dozens of Ero-apps, the phone is able to activate events in real life, fulfilling every sex fantasy of its owner.

Armed with his new powers, Majime seduces girls that would otherwise never even grace him with a look. He builds a harem of horny lovers, head-over-heels for their new master.

Like I have said on many occasions, harem hentai's are often a duel-edged sword for the audience. While having a hentai with a harem gives a wide array of possibility in terms of story, it often has more pressure since know the all of the characters will have to have something that just speaks volumes about them, and this can prove to be tough. However today I'll be talking about a hentai that I feel manages to be a great example at how to handle such a large cast while also having splendid animation and story to go with it. I am of course talking about "Honoo no Haramase Oppai: Ero Appli Gakuen The Animation". Let's jump right in!

Story: 8/10.
The premise is honestly just your run of the mile harem story. Unpopular guy in unpopular until some miracle happens and every girl in school is now willing to suck his 10 inch horse cock... Sorry, got carried away there. Point it the premise well generic gets the job done. And the whole app system is pretty cool, I do like me a mix of some supernatural elements every now and then. The pacing is really what saves the show from falling too out of line in terms of story. In the 2 episodes of the series it makes sure to always build up the characters story and presence just enough so you can get attached but not take time out of the shmex scenes for. Which is something I am sure most people can appreciate.

Art and Animation: 10/10.
Seven is a very interesting hentai studio in that it also produces 'normal anime' as well (a prime example being: Danna ga Nani wo Itteiru ka Wakaranai Ken). This really puts the studio on the map for reputation. Without a doubt Honoo no Haramase Oppai's animation is amazing! The drawings are solid and the coloring used is the perfect mix between the exaggerated tones of hentai and the melancholy of anime (Which makes sense). The animation is also great too! It is very smooth and has a lot of frames put into the animation. The perspective shots during shmex scenes is absolutely amazing too, bringing out the true strength in not only the art and animation but the characters.

Sound: 10/10.
For a studio that does anime production this rating show not come as a surprise. VAs are top notch and so too is the OST.

Character: 9/10.
Honoo no Haramase Oppai knows the importance in cast diversity. It's fine if you make all the girls have huge racks and stacks (Cuz let's be honest that's what most people want) but it is even more important to give the cast a distinctive look from the rest and a bold yet interesting personality. And for the most part, Honoo no Haramase Oppai is AMAZING at this. The cast is among my favorites for a harem. These are only a few of my favorites (Too bad MAL does not have pics of them though): Ayane Uesaka, Belka Valentina, Matsuri Shimano, Yui Hatsunegaoka and Risa Majime. There are some similarities in personalty over the 2 episodes but overall it is a very fresh and unique cast!

Enjoyment: 10/10.
I enjoyed Honoo no Haramase Oppai. The story was generic but it still got what it needed to get done with exceptional pacing at that. The directors really understood what made harem anime so special and elevated that tenfold with the cast. And finally, the art, animation and sound design were absolutely amazing and worth your time!

Overall: 9/10.
Overall Honoo no Haramase Oppai is damn close to being a perfect hentai. The only real gripe I have with the hentai are nitpicks at that and the overall product is great. I would highly recommend that you watch this hentai! It is too good to miss out on!
