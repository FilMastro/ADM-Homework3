Synopsis

It is rumored that a mysterious DVD exists and whoever dares to watch it gets cursed.

The first story centers around Akuno Mika who is in love with Kazuya, a schoolmate. But it seems she is too shy to confess to him. But what happens when she is cursed into a horny demon by the rumored DVD?

The second story is about Kana, who is watching the rumored DVD in request of her scared classmates. But she and her curious little sister Mai have to realize that the rumors are actually true!

Pre-review TLDR:
Just recently seen the 2nd episode for this one and I've seen the 1st episode for it around a year ago and it's a bit blurry for me so i'm going to be mostly talking about the qualities of the 2nd episode here.

Though around 2 years worth of gap between the two episodes releases, the show packs quite a lot of scenes catering to a pretty wide array of fetishes and using some of the non-too common plot devices, mainly a dvd that curses whoever watches them to transform (for the 1st ep) and an aphrodisiac "love potion" circulating throughout the school of magic they're attending.

The good:

• The characters. Again, keep in mind that i'm mostly talking about the 2nd episode here, but be it the fact that the art was done pretty well and that they're really cute, or that because one of them is an elf girl, but they're all pretty lovable in their own way. Especially on how they interacted with one another even if those scenes were pretty rare and short.

• The plot and premise. You don't get a lot of hentais starting with a cursed dvd video turning girls into succubi or twin sisters into futanaris. Though ones with love potions/aphrodisiacs aren't too rare, I still feel like it was handled pretty well here. The plot's not exactly deep, but it's certainly more than enough to keep you from getting bored or too impatient to just skip to the scenes

• The soundtrack. A couple of the tracks stood out for me which is always cool, and also the ending theme left me jamming along with the recap video.

• The art and animation. Like I said earlier, the girls are really cute and the artwork was done pretty well. Animations are pretty good too but could've used juuuust a bit more improvement, but overall it's pretty good.

The bad:

• Not much to say. It's a pretty solid release, even with the huge gap between the episodes, they're still both pretty good and worth watching.

Things that might turn you off:
Futanari
Bondage
Lots of cross-section and x-ray shots

Final Verdict:
As much as I don't completely remember the first ep, it was pretty great from what I remember and the second ep is not too different in that regard. A show that's definitely worth watching if you're into the stuff listed since it caters to a pretty wide array of fetishes and handles them pretty well, paired with good art, cute girls and nice animation. And yeah, Elena best girl.
