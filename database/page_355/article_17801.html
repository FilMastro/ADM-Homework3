Synopsis

Tayu Tayu is an anime adaptation of the adult manga of the same name by Yamatogawa.

Since his parents moved abroad for business, Reiji was forced to live at his old place in the country side. When he decided to enjoy an open air bath, two female childhood friends notice him. That's where the story begins...

I'll start off with the animation since people care so much about it. Queen Bee isn't exactly known for it's top-tier level of animation seen in the likes of Pink Pineapple or Teruaki Murakami. It's basically the poor man's Vanilla series.

So yeah. The next time you hear someone in 4chan bragging about how they'll never "support the industry," just tell 'em that their favorite animu would be done by someone on the level of Queen Bee.

Aside from that; allow me to put "heart-warming" and "sex romp" in the same sentence. Tayu Tayu is a nice heart-warming sex romp about a guy who ends up going back to the countryside, reignites his childhood friendship with two girls, and ends up becoming a human dildo for said two girls and three others.

I'm finding it hard to believe the argument within this series about "hot young men" not being found in the countryside. In the US; you'd find all types of people (along with some "bad-old days" racists and "responsible gun owners") in the countryside. I know it's fantasy, but what about the guys you'd see working in rice fields?
