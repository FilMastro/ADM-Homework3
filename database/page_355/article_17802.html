Synopsis

Hajime Tanaka possesses powers quite unlike that of other students. Thanks to his hypnotic abilities, he can impose his will upon any individual he comes in contact with. He uses this skill to convince a government official to appoint him as a "Sex Counsellor." Consequently, his word is absolute when it comes to sexual matters. Instituted by his new jurisdiction, every couple must undergo counseling under Hajime's watchful eye before engaging in any form of romantic activity.

The first couple to visit Hajime are Yui Obata and Daiki Tachibana. With unwavering hypnosis-induced faith in their Sex Counsellor, Yui subjects herself to the careful inspection of Hajime. Meanwhile, her boyfriend watches them attentively and records their intercourse so as not to miss out on any precious snippet of wisdom.

Although this series is not quite over I decided to make a review on the 2 episodes that were already out, since no one has bothered to yet. Anyways, lets get right into it!

Story: 6.5/10.
Saimin Seishidou honestly had a lot of potential with its premise, potential I felt was a bit wasted. The whole premise revolves around some thick dude who has hypothesis powers and basically fucks a shit ton of girls with it. Yes I know, sounds like bottom of the line hentai, but I truly felt it could have been something better if they didn't go the predictable rape route. If they made the main dude have more personality then I truly felt the story could have been more than the bullshit NTR we always see in hentai. Pacing-wise tho the hentai is good. It knows how to make the most of the 16 minutes and it has a formula which I haven't seen that much in hentai. The formula I am talking about basically consists of the main girl getting fucked, then a bunch of side chicks getting fucked and then finally having one last fuck with the main girl. Although not completely ground breaking it defiantly shows that it works. It gives the audience a nice break while note having to introduce another female lead and over complicating it. Overall, while the premise is really fucking wasted potential the pacing and structure save this part from being absolute dumpster fire.

Art and Animation: 9/10
It's by freaking T-Rex do I really need to say more? This studio has made some of the most aesthetically pleasing hentai ever and Saimin Seishidou is no different! The art style is absolutely amazing with the coloring and contrast really making it pop when watching! Animation is superb as well! The only thing holding it back from being a masterpiece is the mouths... The mouths in this hentai don't match when the characters are talking a lot of the times and it really does distract from the good stuff. Other than that, I have nothing else to say besides... GOD DAMN THAT ART...

Sound: 9/10
Just great! Nothing else to say. Only thing like animation is how the voice don't fit the mouth flaps.

Characters: 7/10
Now this part is weird because I normally do only rate this part based on the girls and if that was the case then I would give a solid 9/10 for the characters. But since all the girls are submissive in this I kind of also have to rate the MC, who is FUCKING SHIT. He literally is the most bland cookie cutter fat ass rapist I have seen. He brings down this whole hentai because he is not original in ANY WAY! But as for the girls they are all great. Even the sidechicks are very enjoyable which I found strange considering they were only in each episode for like 3 minutes. BUT... The fat fuck does ruin the score for this section quite a bit.

Enjoyment: 9/10
Ignoring the main male lead and some animation and sound inconsistencies, this hentai was great! The shmex scenes were amazing and I honestly was really enjoying all of them! The art and animation really were a show stealer and if that is the only reason you watch hentai then you will defiantly not be disappointed in this!

Overall: 8/10. Watch it for the art and animation! But the characters are fairy good too if you ask me!
