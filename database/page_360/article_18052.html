She attends the same class as Hirokazu and for that matter he invited her for a study session and Honoka misunderstood her as his girlfriend. Will Hirokazu make up with his sister in a secret way while Akazaki is still in their house?

When making reviews I try to be as reasonable as I can when I score pieces of art. But there will always be certain works in a medium that completely by pass my rule of thumb. And if we talk about hentai then this is the one series for this medium that I consider one of "my all time favorites" but not the best... Today I'll be reviewing "Ane Kyun!: Joshi ga Ie ni Kita!" or in short: "Ane Kyun". Let's start!

Story: 5/10.
Really there is nothing special about this hentai if I honestly think about it. The story being... well the weakest part. It's basically just an incest story with one of the brother's hot friends assisting as a third wheel of sorts. The story is just plain normal and even though I love this hentai I'll admit. But I feel the pacing is quite good. As the events feel well built up, not perfect by any means though.

Art: 8/10.
In all honesty, Ane Kyun's is super subjective in terms of criticism, you either love it or hate it. Ane Kyun's art is not like "Fela Pure" for instance, which objectively has gorgeous art and animation. Of course I can't blame anyone for not liking Ane Kyun's art as I myself can't really pinpoint why I like it so much. It could be tied to the fact that this is the hentai that got me into hentai. If I were to say a possible reason it could be that the art is perceived in a way that makes the characters more attractive .What do I mean by this? Hentai characters are obviously designed and colored in ways to make them attractive. Most hentai's approach this is a similar way. But Ane Kyun does this differently by making the color feel more dried up and toned down, and for some reason this really resonated with me. Again it is very hard for me to put into words why I like the art. Animation is another story however, as I think it is just "ok". But what really helps the sub-par animation is the directing. In my eyes, the most important thing for any hentai to learn is camera positioning (atleast for shmex scenes). This is so important as, even if you are a low-budget company if you know how to work with what you got then you can making something amazing. I still will argue for the fact that I believe the solo-shmex scene between Akazaki and Hirokazu is one of the best hentai has to offer. Not because of the animation, but the camara angles, which utilizes the art's strengths while giving the animation a helping hand.

Sound: 8/10.
That was a long explanation I apologize. Sound is pretty good. There are sometimes where the audio feels off or the VA messes up but other than that, very solid SFX.

Character: 9/10.
Both Honoka and Akazaki are amazing characters. Both have very appealing designs and personalty wise they are both form an amazing contrast! I personally prefer Akazaki (As she is one of my favorite hentai females) but Honoka is very good as well!

Enjoyment: 10/10.
I think the fact I have seen this hentai more than 25 times proves how much I enjoyed it. If I could I would give this an 11... no 100/10. Ane Kyun definitely has its flaws! But it still means a lot to me and I do think it is objectively good, otherwise I would not have seen it so many times. But it is indefinably a personal favorite, as I don't think this qualifies in being "The Best Hentai of All Time". It is still hella of an amazing one tho!

Overall: 8/10! Watch now and maybe you will feel the same way as me :)
