Synopsis

Shun is the only male teacher at Kyoukasen Girls’ School, a well-known high class school also known as the “Water School” since it is situated in a forest and there is a lake on the school grounds. It is a place to heal the wounds of those girls who are hurt. He meets girls who shut off themselves from the world, those who don’t have confidence in themselves, those who are still traumatized by their past. He decides to help them move forward with their lives.

(Source: VNDB)

History: The Floating Material hentai is based of a dating sim/visual novel. (same name.) It also spun off a fun app on ituns called "My Girl Camera 2".

Art/Characters Designs: The characters aren't anything special. Cardboard cut outs made for your enjoyment. This may not be so bad if they look like a character you've fantasized about before. Both girls & man are attractive- even if the teacher looks younger then the girls. Body positions & background check out okay.

Animation: Choppy. Most of the images are on a loop anyway so I would expect smoother animation. That is what makes the hentai.

Music: Forgettable

Sound: A bit disturbing. During the sex scene the effects didn't sound right. There was a crunching sound that kept playing in and out. Not on accident either. That was the turn off for me.

Voice acting: Great! Well the woman's voice acting was great from them both. The teacher didn't really sound like he was having sex.

Censors?: Yes. Not on boobs but everything ells; yes.

Story/Characters: -To the best of my ability & if you care- Nothing to jump at. A teacher is in a relationship with a student -Ibu. They have all the sex until the last scene. Then there's Himari, a closet pervert who not only has feelings for the teacher or a picture of him on her desk, but likes to spy on the couple & quietly masturbate. In the end the teacher turns invisible and does both girls at the same time.

Overall: You could do better. I'd say unless your a fan or the characters look familiar to other characters you like just pick another hentai

"I watch Hentai for the plot"

That has to be the most retarded claim ever! if you really wanted plot you wouldn't be wasting your time.

So forget all that and let's get into the art.

The art itself is satisfyingly done and there really isn't anything more you could have wanted other than having it uncensored. The censoring is fine, not too blocky, but the art style would have allowed it to be in full detail without it being worse than having it censored. Nevertheless, there is NOTHING bad to report.

Sounds. Like any hentai is going to rely on making a profit from the sound track. Nope, so let's focus on what matters. Voice acting and moaning. Voice acting impressively done and it improves the moaning.

Characters development? Do we have to? NO. Unless it really makes the scenes more passionate and emotional, there really isn't a need for it. The designs are kind of generic, but what the hell, it's still awesome.

Enjoyment. I've only watch ep 1 at the time of the review so I'll just list out all the H scenes and my opinions.

1. Sex in the forest, with the teacher and the long black haired character (no clue what her name is, you'll know who she is if you look at the anime picture).

Seen it before, but very nicely done. Very nice indeed.

2. The pink haired one secretly sees the two from behind a tree, goes to the bathroom and masturbates.

Also something nice to watch.

3. And the last one serves as the great finale with the Black haired one and the sensei doing it in the shower, then going to the bedroom to continue is a nice selection of positions, while the pink haired one hides in the closet masturbating again as the watches.

And that's pretty much it. We didn't get to see all the girls in the picture, but that's a good sign that there's going to be plenty more left where that came from.

It's worth it, it's a keeper.
