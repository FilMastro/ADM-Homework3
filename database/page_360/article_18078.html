Synopsis

Urara starts working at a girls' school. She is now happy because she will get married in a few months. But one day, her fiance suddenly goes missing since his business didn't go well. Months later, Yuji, a debt collector, comes to her house, but she doesn't have enough money to pay. Yuji offers her to work at a boys' school his friend works. He also says if she works there for a month, she won't need to pay back money. She finally decides to accept his offer without knowing the fact that the school is full of evil teachers and students....

This hentai title is about the repeated rape of probably the stupidest woman in the world. If you like rape this one is for you. If you like women that have no brains, then this one is for you. If you want something happy and fun, look elsewhere.

The basic story line is a familiar one in hentai, some woman's fiancee goes missing and a loan shark comes to collect the money. In order to pay off her boyfriend's debt she is told she has to work at a rundown school for a month to pay the interest. Of course when she gets there she starts getting sexually abused by the students. Despite this fact, she keeps going back, all the while apologizing in her mind to the fiancee that screwed her over for the money and ran away. Even when she is repeatedly gang raped she keeps going back, crying, and thinking over and over that she is sorry to the fiancee and thinking she is no longer worthy of him. To be honest, because she is so stupid she deserves everything that happened to her, and I have no pity for her.

So, the artwork is actually very good in this one. The characters are well drawn and appealing. If there was a better story I would have to say that this is a good title, but unfortunately the way that the teacher's story was written ruins it for me. The music got very annoying, it was a non-stop tense whine, not really music at all, just noise to put you further on the edge than you already are.

In conclusion, I am sure that there are plenty of rape fans out there that will love this title. I am not one of those people. I might have enjoyed this if the main character was written so that she had half a brain, but she is not, and stupid people bore and annoy me. So... if you love non-stop rape and despair, then this is the title for you. If not, look elsewhere.
