There are two things that are unique about this hentai. The first is that it is shot in the POV of the male prop of the hentai, the butler, so you only see Kaoru. The second is that the butler never talks, so all you hear is Kaoru. As such, Kaoru is the sole focus and runs the show. Personally, I’m not a big fan of males being a focus in hentai, nor am I a fan of hearing them. As such, these two aspects are a big plus for me in this hentai. As for the specifics:

Story - 5
While at a party, rich spoiled brat Kaoru overhears some girls talking about doing the naughty... She wants to be cool and mature so she enlists the help of her butler to help her do all the things she heard about. Pretty vanilla story but it leads to good banter by Kaoru whose social status allows her to talk down to people. However, considering her social status and the location of the story, a mansion, one would think the setting would be nice during sex scene’s but it’s not. It all takes place in one small room.

Art - 8
I really like Kaoru’s character design. Fit, well endowed, and pink hair. What else could you ask for? She also wears a really nice outfit, only to take it off and reveal an equally nice corset. There is no focus beyond her character design, as the whole hentai is spend in a room with not much other than a bed, chair, and a mirror. Not that I’m complaining. Scene’s are animated well and many sex positions take place throughout the hentai.

Sound - 7
The only thing you hear throughout the whole hentai is Koaru. Copulation sounds are weak as well. However, Koaru’s voice is nice and has that good tsundere sound to it. She puts on a good show and doesn’t shut up once.

Character - 8
The real highlight and main focus of this hentai is the only character in it really - Koaru. She’s a curious about sex tsundere that takes advantage of her power over her butler to have sex with him. However, the tables quickly turn as she transitions from a tsundere who fights every step of the way (even though she’s the one that’s asking for this) to a complete sex freak. Pretty standard character development for a hentai but that’s not a bad thing.

Overall this is a good, tame hentai with a cute tsundere girl who is the center of attention and does not disappoint.

Betsu ni Anta no Tame ni is a cautionary tale in nostalgia, because sometimes those hazy memories hid a much more underwhelming experience that looked much more enticing through rose-tinted glasses and the mystery of something lost to the past. I had actually watched this hentai in my early days, getting into the medium as a young lad, and as I recently became more and more involved with hentai I wished to relive the glory days through some of the first few hentai I had watched, perhaps rekindling those precious feelings to a simpler time in my life. Of the three hentai I remember watching I have found two, this hentai and Itazura the Animation which I had also reviewed, with the latter living up to expectations much greater than this hentai. Betsu ni Anta no Tame ni has been my lesson that sometimes things in the past are best left in the past rather than being dragged through the mud and under a spotlight and today I’ll be breaking down a previously fondly remembered hentai.

Story - 6

So what exactly is BetsuAnta about? Well as the translation of the title “It is not like I grew up like this just for you” suggests it tells the story of a young lady of the tsundere persuasion. This young lady is at a party one night and becomes flustered upon hearing terms such as “fellatio” and “paizuri” thrown around as she is unfamiliar with any and all things sexual. Being so thrown off by what she can only assume is a massive gap in her general knowledge, she calls upon her trusty butler to assist her and obviously this leads to sexual misconduct.

Straight away I was ready to slap a barely passing 5 on the story as it really is quite generic, boring and generally uninteresting. It is the age old story of a guy making fun of a tsundere and her getting understandably frustrated leading into a one-sided game of chicken where the guy eggs the girl on and on. Anyone who’s watched anime can see where this is going from the first few minutes as the tsundere trope and teasing master archetype are quite commonplace so really the only saving grace for the story is that it is taken to certain extremes which bring out an unintentional humorous quality to the two characters. I remember this premise being much more amusing but now seeing it fully, it didn’t have the same oomph that I remembered which is quite disappointing.

Art and Animation - 7.5

We have to cut this hentai some slack obviously, it was released in 2011 and for the time it does quite a serviceable job with the animations not feeling lazy and our main character being appropriately designed to be as tsundere as possible. The backgrounds were also quite good and the ambience of late night sneaking around was captured well creating an environment and mood appropriate to accompany the embarrassing nature of our tsundere’s requests. Visually it satisfied me and honestly reminded me of a better time for hentai, a very much “back in my day” vibe as today’s hentai exhausts me with its subpar artistic direction that feels unfocused and at times lazy.

Characters - 7

There isn’t much to work with here as we really only have one character to judge as you, the main character, are a silent teasing machine and there is no one else but Kaoru the Tsundere. So how does Kaoru do? She’s basically your boilerplate tsundere but her performance does take her to another level and makes you question the limits of your own sanity dealing with the tsundere because actually the MC of this hentai can be whoever you want it to be since this is a rare POV hentai. Kaoru stretches your limits from the cutesy tsundere to the pulling your hair out in frustration tsundere while keeping you hooked with her going along with whatever the fuck you try to pull next. Overall the unique experience of seeing your tsundere from the first person gives this overall bland experience a slight flare aided by the most tsundere thing you have ever seen.

Tsundere Levels - 10

As I have used the term over and over again, yes indeed this is the most tsundere shit I have seen in a hentai. I have never seen stronger mixed signals in anything before as she relentlessly berates you as a massive pervert while also going “What’s next?” which really makes you understand how annoying this shit would be in real life. How else would you define peak tsundere if not a pink haired, twintailed, well-endowed girl saying “It is not like I grew up like this just for you” while continually fucking you, then I don’t know what is. Kaoru switches from anger to love as often as faulty lightbulb, really giving the viewer the purest condensed form of tsundere there has been in a hentai.

Enjoyment - 6.5

If I had watched this hentai for the first time, I would’ve probably given it a higher score like an 8 or a 9 where I would explain that this hentai hit me with a wave of nostalgia that manifested seemingly out of thin air, reminding me of a bygone era of hentai which the new age of hentai barely stacks up to visually. However, I was actually nostalgic for this hentai and had fond memories of it until I accidentally clicked on a link while working on my Shoujo Ramune review leading me to a long, lost memory of my adolescence. Sadly, I did not enjoy this hentai as much as I wanted to since it had been built up so much in my mind recently that the reality shattered my image of it being such a great hentai. At the end of the day, when you think back upon some obscure memory in your past perhaps that’s because it belongs in the past and reliving those memories may not be as good as you remember or may drudge up some less savory details which may ruin said memories. Instead of touching those fond memories of the past, we should all look forward towards a brighter tomorrow filled with new hentai to make new memories with which we shall carry on throughout our lives and perhaps in 20 years we will all be looking back on our degeneracy with a proud smile. However, this hentai was not my proudest fap and while it had strengths such as its focused art direction and unique first-person perspective, it just did not live up to my nostalgia, so for now I'll be putting this hentai back in the box.
