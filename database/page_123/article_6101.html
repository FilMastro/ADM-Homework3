Synopsis

While preparing for a tea party, Hello Kitty receives a distress signal from Haro. She travels to the Universal Century timeline to help Amuro Ray and the Gundam end the One Year War.

Cross-promotion to commemorate the 40th anniversary of Gundam and the 45th anniversary of Hello Kitty.

(Source: ANN)

I'm going to start this review with a statement: This is absolutely the best anime ever made and here's why:

Story: 10
Gundam vs Hello Kitty's story is about Kitty finding out about Ray's struggle in his battle in space. Because of that, she immediately runs to the rescue to stop his enemies through the power of friendship and love in a spectacular battle between love and mechas.

Art: 10
There isn't much to say about the art in this OVA. I think we can all agree on it being absolutely stunning and having probably the greatest animation quality between the latest anime, reaching and surpassing some between the greatest anime movies of all time such as Akira or the more recent Promare.

Sound: 10
The sound design perfectly fits the atmosphere and feel of this piece of history. All the OST has been carefully chosen and composed to bring the experience to new frontiers never reached before.

Character: 10
I don't think there's any need to talk about the main characters as they are two of the most iconic characters in history. As for the supporting cast, although there wasn't much time to develop them, they have a great design and fit perfectly in this kind of story.

Enjoyment: 10
I don't think I've had this much fun watching an anime since I was a little kid. This OVA brang me back to those days and made me feel like a child once again.
Everything in this 6 minutes OVA is absolutely outstanding and I doubt anyone's gonna want to prove me wrong anyway. The general score is absolutely sacrilegious; this masterpiece deserves more recognition.

Using two iconic characters to create a story based about the struggles on maintaining peace around the entire galaxy is absolutely marvelous. As for the characters, using two iconic characters (as stated before) that are loved by many generations, and putting them into an anime is just fucking genius that Studio Sunrise and Sanrio created. Props to them for creating this masterpiece and I highly recommend you to watch this OVA- It doesn't take the long to watch!

This is show is a miracle and I don't regret watching this show at all.
