Satou Reiji's life changed when his real lineage was discovered. He is the son of a rich man who passed away recently, and now Reiji is the new master of the Kamiyama house. But there is a catch! In order for him to be accepted into the house legally, he needs to provide an heir, and in order to do that, he must choose a wife from his very own harem!

Harem Time is almost perfect. There's only a few flaws.

Story: 9

The main story was good but they omitted how Reiji knew Hanazawa who was a regular customer at the place he worked. The side stories are okay but they're completely unnecessary and irrelevant to the story. Calculating the total runtime w/o the side stories would make Harem Time a 30 minute single episode OVA.

Art: 10

The art style was good. I don't care if they're censored or uncensored.

Sound: 10

The voice acting was great.

Character: 9

Some characters aren't given names and the viewers would have a hard time identifying them. As I said earlier, they omitted how Reiji knew Hanazawa. The named girls did their roles well. Out of all the girls, my favorite was Junko.

Enjoyment: 10

I enjoyed Harem Time. It was funny and entertaining. The story, voice acting, art and characters were good.

Overall: 10

Harem Time is an enjoyable hentai that should be recommended to other hentai viewers. It would be nice if it was rereleased as a 30 minute single episode OVA excluding the side stories. It would also be nice if it was dubbed.
